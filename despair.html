Northen Bleakness Recipe

In order to truely recreate the crushing bleakness that comes to the far north of the Edmonton Region 4 out of 12 months of the year, follow below. Guaranteed to make you feel better about where you live currently!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons of Oilers 1st round draft picks
  • 1/2 Cup: Darkness for most of your waking hours
  • 2ft of snow
  • 1 kg of congested traffic on the SW portion of the Henday
  • 1 tsp of Sonic 102.9
  • Dash of Jasper Ave Construction

Steps

  1. Combine all of the above into blender, blitz for eternity
  2. Maybe move to BC?
  3. Relize Edmonton isn't that bad. Our River Valley rocks, we have some great breweries, and the mountains are only 4 hours away ( could be worse, you could live in Saskatchewan or Manitoba )
